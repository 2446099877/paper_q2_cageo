# Draft Highlights for Computers & Geosciences

- Fixed public split manifests and scripted multi-seed aggregation support reproducible low-shot remote sensing evaluation.
- The workflow yields a substantial AID improvement and stable cross-benchmark evidence under identical seed-controlled splits.
- Same-backbone ablations show partial finetuning is the main gain source under the current scarce-label protocol.
- The workflow package includes rebuildable tables, figures, manifests, and auditable experiment records.
- The final workflow remains trainable on a single 8 GB GPU with only about 7.5M trainable parameters.
